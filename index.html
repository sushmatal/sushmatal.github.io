Spring Transits

Spring Transits is a company that aspires to enable women in a very unique and sustainable way, one cohort at a time, to pursue fulfilling technical careers in a flexible remote work setup. We build an overarching ecosystem for our cohorts to develop their skills, regain confidence, and pivot as needed. We match our people with mentors who help them grow exponentially through knowledge sharing and one-on-one coaching. Our grooming philosophy helps build a culture where a group of individuals gains experience in a safe and flexible setting. We team with our partners to work on real products and services helping our cohorts gain valuable experience in the marketplace. Our mission is to help women get back to the workforce with incredible confidence and right skills, we also want to be recognized as a company that deeply cares about the process of grooming individuals.

About Us

About the Company

Spring Transits enables group of women, co-located in a community, pursuing technical careers, and need flexible working hours to restart, rebuild and sustain technical skills to stay relevant in the dynamic tech industry. Our mission is to build confidence, skills and readiness in our cohorts. We offer an eco-system to our cohorts that pairs them with mentors, connects with local tech groups, enrich learning through product development and services with industry partners.

Our belief

  • While we help women in technology find their passion and time to reenter workforce, we can build an extraordinary software development company with these people by investing time and resources in a flexible and remote work culture.

Our Commitment

  • We provide the help our cohorts need to acquire skills required to build long careers in the tech industry through a series of coaching and mentoring sessions.
  • Our cohorts will work on a real product idea aiding small local businesses and build this to gain experience in software development life cycle.
  • With the help of an amazing network of our cohorts and mentors, we will work with you to address all your software development needs!
